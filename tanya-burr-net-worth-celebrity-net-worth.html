What is Tanya Burr's Net Worth?

Tanya Burr is an English vlogger, blogger, author, and make-up artist who has a net worth of $2 million. Tanya Burr was born in Norwich, Norfolk, England in June 1989. She is best known for having her own fashion and beauty channel on YouTube. Burr released her cosmetics line Tanya Burr Cosmetics in 2014.

Her self-titled YouTube channel was created in 2009 and she started doing make-up tutorials centered around how to look like celebrities. Her YouTube channel has more than three million subscribers and more than 240 million views. Her autobiography and non-fiction beauty guide Love, Tanya was published in 2015. Tanya married YouTube star and vlogger Jim Chapman in 2015.
