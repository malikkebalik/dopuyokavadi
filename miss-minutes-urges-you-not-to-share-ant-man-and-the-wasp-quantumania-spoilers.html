Miss Minutes urges you not to share Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania spoilers

Publish date: 2024-08-31

We’re just days away from the premiere of Ant-Man and The Wasp: Quantumania and the official debut of the Marvel Cinematic Universe’s next Big Bad, Kang. But the best little helper from his incarnation of He Who Remains is still taking her job seriously. Miss Minutes from Loki is back and she’s here to tell all of y’all to hush up when it comes to spoilers.

Recommended Videos

As it happens, the Time Variance Authority’s mascot and virtual concierge has her very own Twitter account and the latest tweet is a warning to “keep those timelines clean” because others “haven’t been approved to view the timeline yet.” It’s not exactly a stretch to figure out that in her own special way, Miss Minutes is reminding all of us to keep our spoilers offline and private until Quantumania hits theaters (and preferably for a while afterward).

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Loki (@officialloki)

The post seems to indicate that Miss Minutes’ duties may be expanding past the approved TVA timeline and into the other realms and dimensions of the multiverse. This makes sense given that the multiverse was unleashed in the final episode of Loki season one when Sylvie killed He Who Remains. Miss M has always had a protective relationship with He Who Remains so perhaps she’s just expanding her authority a bit.

There’s no telling if this indicates we’ll actually see Miss Minutes appear in Quantumania. Voice actor Tara Strong, who provides Miss Minutes’ southern drawl, isn’t on the cast list — but that’s no guarantee that she won’t show up in an Easter egg or surprise appearance. In any case, fans will definitely see and hear her again when Loki season two is released later this year.

In the meantime, keep your timeline clean.
