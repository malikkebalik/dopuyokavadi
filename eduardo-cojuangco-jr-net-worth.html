What is Eduardo Cojuangco, Jr.'s Net Worth?

Eduardo Cojuangco, Jr. if a Filipino businessman and politician who has a net worth of $1.4 billion. Eduardo Cojuangco Jr. was born in Paniqui, Tarlac, Philippine Islands in June 1935. He is the chairman of San Miguel Corporation which is the largest food and beverage corporation in the Philippines and Southeast Asia. Cojuangco served as the Governor of Tarlac from 1967 to 1969 and as a Member of the Philippine House of Representatives from Tarlac's First District from 1969 to 1972.

At one time it was estimated that Eduardo Cojuangco, Jr.'s business empire accounted for 25% of the gross national product of the Philippines. He was a candidate for the Philippine presidency in 1992 but finished second. He founded the Nationalist People's Coalition in 1992 and serves as chairman emeritus.
