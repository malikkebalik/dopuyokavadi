Kubota B3200 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V1505 diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 91.5 ci [1.5 L] engine, which delivers 32 hp [23.9 kW] power at rated rpm 2700.

Kubota B3200 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.5L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, wet disc brakes, 1,655 lbs [750 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 1,764 lbs [800 kg] weight, and 5.7 Qts [5.4 L] oil capacity.

Kubota B3200 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a 60″ Mower deck, 72″ Mower deck, Kubota LA504 loader, and Kubota BH76 backhoe.
