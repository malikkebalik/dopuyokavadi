What is Clarence Seedorf's Net Worth?

Clarence Seedorf is a Dutch professional soccer player and manager who has a net worth of $20 million. Clarence Seedorf was born in Paramaribo, Suriname in April 1976. He has been called one of the best midfielders of his generation. He played his youth career for VV AS '80, Read Almere, and Ajax. Seedorf made his senior career debut for Ajaz in 1992 and scored 11 goals in 65 appearances through 1995. He played for Sampdoria from 1995 to 1996. He scored 15 goals in 121 caps for Real Madrid from 1996 to 2000. Seedorf played for Internazionale from 2000 to 2002.

He scored 47 goals in 3000 games for Milan from 2002 to 2012 and finished his career with Botafogo from 2012 to 2014. He also represented the Netherlands in international competition from 1994 to 2008 scoring 11 goals in 87 caps. In 2014 Seedorf managed Milan. He is the only player to win the Champions League with three different clubs and was chosen as part of the FIFA 100 by Pele in 2004. Seedorf was named Dutch Football of the Year in 1993 and 1994. He is part of the A.C. Milan Hall of Fame.
