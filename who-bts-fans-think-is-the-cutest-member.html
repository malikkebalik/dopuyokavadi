Who BTS Fans Think Is the Cutest Member

Publish date: 2024-07-05

The BTS members are known for their award-winning music, but they’ve also earned recognition for their visuals. When it comes to Aegyo and the cutest members of this K-pop group, there are a few performers who earn recognition from fans. 

Here’s what some ARMYs are saying on Quora. 

RM

Sure, he’s a rapper and a dancer with a lot of swag, but he also loves going on walks with his dog Rapmon and taking care of bonsai trees. Some BTS fans also consider RM to be one of the cutest members. 

“The cutie-pie leader, who happens to be obsessed with bonsai trees, Ryan the bear, art museums,” one Quora user wrote. “And did I mention that he has what seem to be the KEEYUTEST DIMPLES like EVER!”

Jimin

He’s BTS’ “Resident Prince Charming,” but the singer and dancer, Jimin, is also super cute. That could be because he’s the shortest member of BTS or just because he often sports brightly colored hair. 

“Jimin is also considered super attractive,” one Quora user said. “He was very insecure about his cheeks and weight before, but I think he’s better about them now especially after he went on his diet to lose weight. I think he’s the most self-conscious about his looks, although he really doesn’t need to be.”

Suga

Although he could appear savage and stoic, the rapper and dancer, Suga, also has his sweet and cute moments. That even earned him his “Lil Meow Meow” nickname from some members of the BTS ARMY. 

“First of all, Yoongi is the biggest uwu I have ever seen,” one Quora user wrote. “Jimin is known for being the target of the boys’ small jokes, (because he’s small,) but have you guys noticed — YOONGI IS SMALL AS WELL! Maybe they don’t direct their jokes at him because, well, it’s Yoongi.”

Jungkook

He’s the youngest member of BTS, growing from a teenager to an adult with the other members. Although he’s an adult and Time Magazine’s “Sexiest International Man” of 2020, some fans still know him for being adorable. 

“The fact that we all have watched him grow up is something that I find strange, but it’s that change from a boy to a young adult that makes you able to see how much his looks, face have changed,” one Quora user wrote. “He eludes such an adult, childlike innocence to him, which is so interesting.”
