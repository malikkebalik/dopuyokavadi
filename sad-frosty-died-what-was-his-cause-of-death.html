The hip-hop world is in mourning following the announcement of the death of Sad Frosty, a rapper and social media influencer whose career was on the rise.

Frosty, born in Houston, Texas, on April 3, 1997, died on Friday, January 14, at just 24 years old, as announced on his official Instagram account.

“Long live Sad Frosty 3/4/97 – 1/14/22”, reads the publication of the singer’s official account along with some photos of the artist, whose real name is Sam Hernandez.

The news moved his thousands of followers and fans, who left many messages of condolence.

“I can’t believe this shit bro “, “I love you man… you really helped a lot of people more than you think “, “Rest in peace”, “Rest in peace. Young legend”, they said.

According to The New York Post, so far the official cause of death of the young rapper is unknown. However, the rapper Quadeca, who was very affected by the news, said that it could have been an overdose.

“Sorry this is hard. RIP SAD FROST. I’m really going to miss you bro,” he said in a video on his YouTube channel.

Little is known of Frosty’s personal life, including his real name.

For what he did gain acclaim from many rap fans, it was for his hits “Crib With a Lake” and “New Kicks.” “ADHD Freestyle” is the title of the song with which Frosty burst onto the music scene.
