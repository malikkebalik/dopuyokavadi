Colin Donnell is an American actor who recently started his Hollywood career with a role on the short-lived series Pan Am. Afterwards, he got the role of Tommy Merlyn on Arrow, being part of the main cast on Season 1. After Arrow, he continued his TV career with guest appearances on Person of Interest and Unforgettable before gaining recognition for his role as Scotty Lockhart on The Affair. As of 2015, he stars as Dr. Connor Rhodes on Chicago Med. Around the same time, he also made his big screen debut in the film Every Secret Thing.
