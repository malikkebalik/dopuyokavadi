What is Erika de la Rosa's Net Worth?

Erika de la Rosa is a Mexican actress and model who has a net worth of $4 million. Erika de la Rosa was born in Ciudad Juarez, Chihuahua, Mexico in March 1980. Her first role came in 2003 in the television series Gardener's Daughter. She next appeared in the series Life is a Song in 2004 and Montecristo from 2006 to 2007. De la Rosa appeared in the TV series Se Busca Un Hombre and Lo que callamos las mujeres in 2007. In 2008 she starred in the series Change of life and Forbidden desire.

She next starred in the series Cada quien su santo and Pasion Morena in 2009. In 2011 she starred in the television series Empress and in the series The Landlady in 2013. In 2014 Erika de la Rosa starred in the TV series The Lord of the Skies. She also starred in the 2013 film What did you say to God?. De la Rosa also appeared in a theater production of Five women wearing the same dress in 2010.
